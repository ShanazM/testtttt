The Adventures of Sang Kancil - English in Wonderland

Deep in the tropical rainforest of Malaysia, a clever mouse-deer named Sang Kancil woke up feeling famished. He needed food and water but couldn't find anything nearby. As he searched, he came to a murky river. On the other side, he saw delicious red fruits under a tree.

But how could he cross the river? Sang Kancil noticed some crocodiles swimming nearby. He hatched a plan!

What should Sang Kancil do?

Ask the crocodiles to help him. Try to swim across alone.

Sang Kancil called out to the crocodiles: “Hello, my friends! King Solomon sent me to count all the crocodiles in this river. Please line up so I can count you!”

The crocodiles, afraid of King Solomon, quickly formed a line across the river. Sang Kancil jumped across their backs, counting loudly as he went.
